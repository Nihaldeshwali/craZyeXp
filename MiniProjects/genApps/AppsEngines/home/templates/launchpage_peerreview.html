World-class mock interviewing experience for students and professionals

The PeerReview provides a professional one-on-one opportunity to practice your coding interview. By doing so, we will challenge yourself to keep thinking even after you have a first solution. See how many solutions you can come up with. This will grow your ability to quickly see multiple ways to solve a problem, so you can figure out the best solution. This is help to get you confidence while real interview, by increasing problem solving skill, improving though process and finally ending with professional coding!
